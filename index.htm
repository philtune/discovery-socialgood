Emergency Impact

Discovery responds in times of disaster and crisis around the world to raise awareness.

We partner with organizations to provide hands-on support to rebuild communities.

Housing

Discovery works with organizations such as Habitat For Humanity.

Discovery works with organizations such as Habitat For Humanity who helps families rebuild and improve places to call home. As a long-standing partner, we have worked with Habitat for Humanity in communities impacted by inequality and disaster to help families build a better life for themselves.

Education

At Discovery, we power the passions that make us human.

But many people lack the opportunity to pursue those passions in everyday life. Through RISE, we are committed to reducing inequality and supporting empowerment to help improve social mobility and provide access to basic human needs and education programs for underserved populations around the world.

Hunger

1 in 4 kids in America lives with hunger.

1 in 4 kids in America lives with hunger. Childhood hunger is a problem we know how to solve. There is plenty of food in this country and programs to feed kids, but too many families do not have access. Turn Up! Fight Hunger, a partnership between No Kid Hungry and Discovery, engages audiences to help provide one billion meals to kids living with hunger in the U.S. over the next five years.

Conservation

Discovery is committed to the conservation, preservation and restoration of our planet and its wildlife.

Through Project C.A.T., we are supporting a global effort to double the number of tigers in the wild tiger by 2022 to ensure a healthy future for generations of tigers and other endangered species.

Democracy

Our collective voices have the power to make real change.
